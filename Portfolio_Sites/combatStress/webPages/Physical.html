Mind's Reaction

Cognitive Symptoms of stress is the way we mentally process our surroundings.

  • difficult concentrating
  • Easily distracted by worries
  • tendency to be negative, self-defeating, or perfectionist thinking
  • Anxious
  • bad judgment
  • difficulty remembering
  • Moodiness
  • Agitation
  • Feeling overwhelmed
  • Sense of loneliness and isolation
  • Depression

Body's Reaction!

Behavioral symptoms of stress are actions performed that are observed by other people.Physiological symptoms of stress are the response of the body to stressful situations.

  • shallow, rapid breathing
  • heart racing
  • increased perspiration
  • nausea or stomachache
  • increased muscle tension
  • headache, backache
  • tightness in chest
  • dry mouth
  • increased urge to urinate
  • cold hands or feet
