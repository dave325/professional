Stress is perfectly normal. It is just a sense of danger - real or imaginary - that kicks your body's defenses into high gear, also known as "fight or flight" or the stress response. Stress has a positive effect on your body. It helps you stay energetic, focused, and alert.



Physiological symptoms of stress are the response of the body to stressful situations.



Cognitive Symptoms of stress is the way we mentally process our surroundings.



Stress is easily manageable, a few ways are: the use of technology, learning to say "no", setting realistic goals, etc.. Keepinga positive outlook on life is the key.
