I am here to give you the
One-on-one service you and your child deserve.

The teenage years are one of the most difficult times in a person's life. There are so many challenges your child is facing right now, and s/he can use my help to be successful. Parents should be better equipped to guide their children through high school, college and life beyond. However, you may not be aware of the science and the know-how behind the high school and college process. I can guide your child through this process and I am confident that he or she will be successful. My services are convenient and affordable. I am available to come to you during evenings and on Saturdays to provide you with the following services and more: graduation status review, transcript interpretation, and college advising, and counseling. Together we can create the path to your child's success. I look forward to serving you and see your child succeed.
