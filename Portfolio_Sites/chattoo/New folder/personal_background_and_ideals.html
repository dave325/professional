PERSONAL BACKGROUND AND IDEALS

I am Indian-American and lived in Queens for over 30 years, which has allowed me to learn about so many diverse cultures and various backgrounds. I was fortunate enough to have loving parents who instilled in me the value of education, and they poured their life and soul into me to make me the man I am today. I became a mental health professional and educator to give back to my community the same way in which I was nourished. My goal is to make a difference in teenager’s lives and I want to make this city a better place than which I found it.

Being an American from South Asian backgrounds I can relate to a wide and diverse group of people. I find that many common issues underlie almost all different groups. Life brings on many challenges, and I am here to help you to meet and deal with these challenges. I am a practical, interactive, solution-focused professional whose treatment approach is to provide support and practical feedback to help clients resolve current problems and long-standing patterns. With sensitivity and compassion, I help clients find solutions to those problems and patterns. Blending conventional and alternative approaches, I draw on a variety of styles and techniques to incorporate what will be most helpful for each client. I work with each client to help build on his or her strengths to identify and achieve life goals.
